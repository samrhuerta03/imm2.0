Samantha Huerta

CEO & Main programmer

I will be starting junior year in the fall of 2019 at St.Catharine Academy situated in the Bronx. I am looking forward to all my courses, especially the 4 AP’s I thought was a good idea to sign up for. Partaking in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program has allowed me to learn more about coding and the various professions that use it, which has increased my interest in pursuing a career as a software engineer. I am very grateful for the knowledge, opportunities, and friendships the program has offered me.

Being a first generation daughter of immigrant parents has allowed me to see first hand the struggles and discrimination my parents experience. It is because of those struggles that i am deeply connected with this project. With the help of my other 4 teammates, we were able to create a website that helps everyone in the United States. As the main programmer I was in charge of creating the backbone of the website, design aspect of the home page and creators page, and assisting my teammates in regards to the website code. As a CEO of the website, the two other CEO’s and I created the idea of this website and were in charge of the setup/design aspect of the overall website.
