Edilia Bueno

CEO & Main researcher

My name is Edilia Bueno. I currently live in Mount Vernon, NY and a rising senior at Sacred Heart Greenwich. I was born and raised in a beautiful tropical island, the Dominican Republic. My mother and I immigrated to the United States 2006. It was a challenge to adjust the culture here. We did not know out rights, or support on getting myself an education. This website captures a hope for your protection. It will help you gain knowledge on information that the government will not tell you. This will give you a chance to give your children a better education. I know how it feels to have nothing or not having anyone’s support. However, my team and I have confidence that you will be able to defeat your destiny. Anything is possible if you have faith.

My name is Samantha Huerta. I will be a rising junior in the fall of 2019 at St.Catharine Academy, situated in the Bronx. I am looking forward to all my courses, especially the 4 AP’s I thought was a good idea to sign up for.
